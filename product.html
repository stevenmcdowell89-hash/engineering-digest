Agentic Trust Rails, the First New UK Payment Scheme Since 2008, and the Wallet's Bid for Loyalty

12 June 2026 · covering 13 May – 12 June 2026
The month's biggest moves were about new rails, not new value. Visa and Mastercard specified the trust layer for agent-led checkout on a single June day — with loyalty nowhere in it. The UK switched on its first new payment scheme since 2008, and the first products on it target recurring spend. Apple's iOS 27 turned the wallet into a recruiter of loyalty cards. And Lloyds, Chase and Nationwide gave three different answers to what a reward is for.
